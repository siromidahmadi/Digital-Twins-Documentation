Key Features

Flexible Project Integration

Add and manage projects across disciplines, including software, AI, architectural models, simulations, or research studies.

Interactive Visualization & Tracking

Monitor project progress with dashboards, visual milestones, and interactive elements like 3D models, diagrams, Rhino/Grasshopper scripts, or Ladybug environmental simulations.

Collaboration & Guidance

Connect with team members, professors, and contributors; receive contextual instructions, tips, or project recommendations.

Tool Integration

Supports integration with Ladybug for environmental analysis, Rhino for 3D modeling, and Grasshopper for parametric design and automation.

Problem It Solves

Projects in fields like architecture, engineering, or computer science are often scattered across different tools, formats, and teams, making collaboration and progress tracking difficult. This platform centralizes project data, standardizes formats, and provides interactive tracking and visualization, so teams and stakeholders can collaborate effectively and make informed decisions.

Data Type It Works With

  • Code & Scripts: Python, Java, C#, Rhino/Grasshopper scripts
  • 3D Models & Diagrams: Rhino, Grasshopper, CAD, OBJ, STL, Revit
  • Simulation & Environmental Data: Ladybug outputs, sensor logs, CSV/TSV files
  • Media & Reports: Images, videos, PDFs, presentations
  • Time Series / Analytics: Telemetry, experimental measurements, parametric design outputs
